③ 短文閱讀 GSAT reading

Many people believe that talent is the main reason for success. However, daily effort often matters more. A student who reviews vocabulary for ten minutes every day may gradually develop a stronger sense of language than someone who studies only before a test.

Learning becomes more effective when it is connected to real life. For example, students can write short English sentences about their school, family, hobbies, or plans. In this way, English is not just a subject to memorize, but a tool to express ideas.

顯示中文翻譯

許多人相信天分是成功的主要原因。然而，每天的努力往往更重要。每天複習十分鐘單字的學生，可能逐漸培養出比只在考前讀書的人更強的語感。

當學習與真實生活連結時，效果會更好。例如，學生可以用英文寫關於學校、家庭、興趣或計畫的短句。這樣一來，英文不只是要背誦的科目，而是表達想法的工具。

④ 今日小測驗 3 questions

1. The teacher tried to ________ the importance of daily review.
A. ignore　B. emphasize　C. damage　D. borrow
2. It is important ________ students to connect English with real life.
A. to　B. of　C. for　D. at
3. 翻譯：這個問題太複雜了，我們需要更多時間討論。
顯示答案

1. B emphasize
2. C for
3. This issue is so complex that we need more time to discuss it.
