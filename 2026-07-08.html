① 今日核心單字 10 words

② 今日句型 2 patterns

make good use of + N：善用……
We should make good use of our free time.
我們應該善用空閒時間。
never ... without V-ing：每次……一定會……
I never start my morning without drinking a cup of coffee.
我每天早上開始時一定會喝一杯咖啡。

③ 短文閱讀 GSAT reading

Many students think they need a long period of time to improve their English. In fact, small daily actions can make a big difference. For example, learning a few useful words, reading one short paragraph, and listening to one sentence carefully can gradually build a strong foundation.

The key is not perfection but consistency. When students review a little every day, they become more familiar with sentence patterns and common words. Over time, English becomes less stressful and more natural.

顯示中文翻譯

許多學生以為他們需要很長一段時間才能改善英文。事實上，每天小小的行動就能帶來很大的差別。例如，學幾個實用單字、讀一小段文章、仔細聽一句英文，都能逐漸建立穩固的基礎。

關鍵不是完美，而是持續。當學生每天複習一點，他們會更熟悉句型和常見單字。久而久之，英文會變得比較不緊張，也更自然。

④ 今日小測驗 3 questions

1. Good planning makes study time more ________.
A. efficient　B. nervous　C. foreign　D. heavy
2. We should make good use ________ our free time.
A. at　B. of　C. on　D. for
3. 翻譯：我每天早上開始時一定會喝一杯咖啡。
顯示答案

1. A efficient
2. B of
3. I never start my morning without drinking a cup of coffee.
