③ 短文閱讀 頂標取向

In an age of information, students do not simply need more facts. They need the ability to judge whether information is useful, reliable, and relevant. This skill is especially important because false or incomplete information can spread quickly online.

One practical strategy is to ask three questions before accepting a claim: Who wrote it? What evidence is provided? Does another reliable source say the same thing? By doing so, students can become more careful readers and more responsible users of information.

顯示中文翻譯

在資訊時代，學生需要的不只是更多事實。他們需要判斷資訊是否有用、可靠且切題的能力。這項能力特別重要，因為錯誤或不完整的資訊可能在網路上快速傳播。

一個實用策略是在接受某個說法前先問三個問題：誰寫的？提供了什麼證據？是否有另一個可靠來源也這樣說？透過這樣做，學生能成為更謹慎的讀者，也能更負責任地使用資訊。
